Who We Are

Welcome to Head Start School. We are an English- medium, ultra-modern, co-educational school with multi-cultural enrolment.
We provide a happy, secure and safe environment that enables learners to actively participate in their own learning.
Our multi-sensory approach to child development and training ensures a flexible Individual Education Plan for each child under our care.

Student Life/Co-curricular Activities

Our Students are taken through extra-curricular activities as they are an essential element of their educational experience. Over the years, these non-academic
activities such as sports and debate clubs have proven to help our students learn about themselves, develop and use their skills and knowledge in different contexts.
